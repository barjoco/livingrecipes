Bringing the freshest, tastiest Middle Eastern recipes to your door

Our Values: The freshest, healthiest food at the lowest impact on our planet.

Living recipes has been created to give busy people , the freshest, healthiest and tastiest meal packed full of Middle Eastern flavours, in a box delivered to your door, and guaranteed to stay fresh until you are ready to make it.

All the ingredients are provided along with a recipe card to allow you to make a really health, tasty meal for 2 from the freshest ingredients, with all delicate herbs delivered still living to keep them as fresh as possible.

How it works

.

  1. You select a recipe from the Living Recipe page
  2. You order (a small deposit is payable on the first order to ‘buy’ your packaging. Packaging will then be recycled or re used for subsequent orders)
  3. We deliver all the ingredients to your door in our 100% recycled packaging, together with the recipe card
  4. You make your Middle Eastern meal on the day of your choice knowing the living herbs will be as fresh as the day they were delivered.
  5. You put out ALL the packaging from the Living Recipes box for us to collect and recycle when we deliver your next box.
